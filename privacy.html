1. Overview

At Weekenderly, your privacy matters to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use our platform and services. We are committed to handling your data with the highest standards of transparency and responsibility.

By accessing or using Weekenderly, you agree to the terms outlined in this policy.

2. Information We Collect

We collect minimal user data necessary to provide our community service. This may include:

  • Name: To personalise your experience and facilitate introductions within the community.
  • Contact Information: Email address and/or phone number for community access, invitations, and communication.
  • City/Location: To match you with relevant local weekend experiences.
  • Preferences: Your weekend interests (e.g., caf√©s, treks, events) to provide curated recommendations.

3. How We Use Your Information

Your data is used exclusively for:

  • Granting and managing your community access
  • Sending you curated weekend experience invitations
  • Matching you with like-minded members in your city
  • Communicating updates, new features, and important announcements
  • Improving our platform and services based on usage patterns

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal data to any third party. Your information is never shared for advertising or commercial purposes without your explicit consent.

We may share limited, anonymised data with trusted service providers (e.g., communication platforms) solely to facilitate community operations.

5. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organisational measures to protect your personal data from unauthorised access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. Your data is stored securely and access is restricted to authorised personnel only.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data at any time
  • Opt out of community communications
  • Withdraw your consent to data processing

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at hello@weekenderly.com.

7. Cookies

Our website may use cookies to enhance your browsing experience. These are small files stored on your device that help us remember your preferences. You can disable cookies through your browser settings, though this may affect certain functionality.

8. Changes to This Policy

We reserve the right to update this Privacy Policy periodically. Any changes will be communicated via email or a prominent notice on our website. Continued use of our services after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
